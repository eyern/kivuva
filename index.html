Dr. Antony Kivuva

A Neurosurgeon in Mombasa, Kenya

Dr Antony Kivuva is an exceptional Neurosurgeon & Neurologist in Mombasa with expertise in treating brain, nerve, spine conditions. He specialises in adult and paediatric neurology & spine surgery. As a Brain & Spine Surgeon, he utilises the latest surgical techniques to provide effective treatments. He empasises providing patient-centred care and explaining diagnoses and treatment options to his patients.
Dr Antony Kivuva also treats brain and spine conditions, including seizures, multiple sclerosis, brain tumours, spinal cord injuries and spinal deformities. He is committed to providing individualised treatment plans to meet your medical needs. He is also a member of East African Association of Neurosurgeons.
